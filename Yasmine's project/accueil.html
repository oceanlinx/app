Our Vision

Our vision is to revolutionize the global shipping industry, enabling efficient, secure, and sustainable trade. We will modernize global shipping for a smarter, stronger, and more resilient global economy.
Since the dawn of time, trade has been essential to human civilization. The advent of container shipping was a watershed moment, unlocking significant economic value and benefits as the world globalized.
Despite the evolution of global shipping, other industries have transformed through technology at a much faster pace. Information remains on paper and is exchanged using slow, manual methods, costing the industry over $30 billion annually. As a result, the global shipping industry struggles to keep up with the rising complexity of supply chains, markets, and economies.
Our online platform will serve as the global trade "operating system" of choice, beginning with the shipping industry. By leveraging blockchain technology, our platform will enable trusted cooperation between diverse and competing market participants, resulting in increased efficiencies and resilience.

Our Platform

OceanLinx is a comprehensive service platform designed to streamline and enhance maritime shipping operations connecting European countries, including the UK, Ireland, Spain, Portugal, and France, with their North American counterparts, the United States and Mexico. This platform acts as a crucial link between these regions, offering a range of shipping services to ensure efficient and cost-effective transportation across the Atlantic Ocean.
OcenaLinx will allow businesses to compare quotes from multiple service providers, book their shipment, and track its progress in real time. We will also provide a variety of features to help businesses manage their shipments more effectively, such as the ability to upload shipping documents, generate invoices, and track their shipping budget.
We believe that our platform can play a significant role in improving the efficiency and transparency of the sea freight shipping industry. We are committed to providing our customers with the best possible experience, and we are excited to help businesses grow their businesses through international trade.
